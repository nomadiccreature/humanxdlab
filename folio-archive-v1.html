Hi 👋🏼,

I’m a User Experience designer with experience designing services and products across diverse domains such as healthcare, archives, finance, insurance, property and education.

I've been working in the digital space for over a decade. So far I've worked on projects that involved technologies such as AI/ML, Big Data, WebRTC, Voice, AR/VR, Distributed ledger (Blockchain), Dashboards and Search.

I am skilled in User Research, Service Design, Interaction Design, Information Architecture, User Interface (UI) Development, Project Management, Stakeholder Management and Compliance by Design.​

As a design practitioner, I am both strategic and hands-on. I have led end-to-end journey of services, shipped multiple (end-to-end design) products managing it from initial discovery (concept) to final deliverables in an agile environment; managed teams of designers and developers; negotiated with senior stakeholders as well as mentored junior colleagues and ran design events for underrepresented groups. Due to my computer science background, I'm also familiar with the coding jargons and the execution mindset.



I have completed three (3) Masters degree, my last one was at University College London (UCL) with a major in ​Human-Computer Interaction​ (HCI), where I did an extensive study of theories and principles in topics such as design practice, multimedia technologies, computing, cognitive psychology, ergonomics and business models.

Due to my experiences, I approach a problem statement from a holistic perspective by understanding business economics, empathising with the user needs, and analysing technical feasibility.

Design Ethos:

User-Centered // Data-Driven // Agile // Inclusive // Empathy // Organic
