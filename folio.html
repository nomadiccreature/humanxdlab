Arindra Das

Design | Research | Product | Strategy





I’m a design leader and strategist with a focus on complex and highly regulated environments. My practice spans service design, interaction design, product strategy and research, helping organisations navigate complexity and create inclusive, resilient services.

My work includes zero to one launches, large-scale redesigns and critical MVPs. I have led design strategy and delivery for national programmes, scaling design systems, building teams and working with senior leaders across policy, product and technology. Alongside this I stay close to the craft, prototyping, mapping journeys, evolving design systems and grounding decisions in research. I also bring experience designing for algorithmic and automated systems, where the challenge is to make data-driven services transparent, usable and trusted.

I hold a Master’s in Human–Computer Interaction from UCL and began my career in engineering, which gives me a grounding in both technical systems and human-centred approaches.
